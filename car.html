The Lamborghini Huracán is a high-performance sports car produced by the Italian automaker Lamborghini. It was first introduced in 2014 as a successor to the Lamborghini Gallardo. The name "Huracán" is Spanish for "hurricane," and it follows Lamborghini's tradition of naming their cars after famous fighting bulls.










The Lamborghini Urus is a luxury SUV produced by the Italian automaker Lamborghini. It was first introduced in 2017 and marked Lamborghini's re-entry into the SUV market, following the Lamborghini LM002, which was produced in the late 1980s and early 1990s. The Urus is designed to offer a combination of high-performance, comfort, and versatility, making it a unique addition to Lamborghini's lineup.











The Lamborghini Vision Gran Turismo is a concept car developed by the Italian automaker Lamborghini for the popular video game series Gran Turismo. The concept car was created specifically for the Gran Turismo Sport video game and was first unveiled in 2019. While it was initially designed as a digital-only vehicle for the game, Lamborghini later built a physical model of the car for display at various automotive events.











The Lamborghini Essenza SCV12 is an extremely limited-production, track-only hypercar developed by the Italian automaker Lamborghini. It was officially unveiled in July 2020. This hypercar is part of Lamborghini's "Lamborghini Squadra Corse" division, which focuses on creating high-performance racing cars and special limited-edition models for the track
